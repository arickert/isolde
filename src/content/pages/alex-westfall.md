_The Weight of Light_ is a new body of painting by Alex Westfall, exploring color, luminosity, and perception.

Westfall works primarily in oil on linen, building surfaces through layers of translucent glazes that shift depending on ambient light conditions.

Opening reception: Sunday, May 3, 4–7pm
