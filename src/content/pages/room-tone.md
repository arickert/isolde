*Room Tone* was a solo exhibition by Kai Nakamura exploring sound, silence, and the architecture of listening.

Nakamura's practice spans sculpture, sound installation, and text scores. This was their first exhibition in New York.

Opening reception: Sunday, June 8, 4–7pm
