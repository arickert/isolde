*Soft Architecture* presents new sculptural and photographic work by Ariel West, investigating the relationship between the body and built environments.

West's practice spans installation, photography, and text. This exhibition marks her first solo presentation in New York.

Opening reception: Sunday, March 8, 4–7pm
